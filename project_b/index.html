A Taste of Guatemala

Shuco is one of the most delicious street food in Guatemala. It is a perfect meal for birthday parties, walking around parks, and late night snacks. Shuco means "dirty" in Guatemala, and they called it this because of the amount of ingredients it has and how messy it is to eat it. The best part is that you can choose what ingredients to add.

    Ingredients:

  • Guacamole
  • Chorizo or Sausage
  • Chicken
  • Mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
