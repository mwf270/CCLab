A Taste of Guatemalan Food

Shuco is one of the most delicious street food in Guatemala. It is a perfect meal for birthday parties, late night snacks, or on walks around the park. Shuco means "dirty" in Guatemala, and they called it this because of the amount of ingredients it has and how messy it is to eat it.

    Ingredients:

  1. Guacamole
  2. Sausage
  3. Chicken
  4. Mayonnaise
  5. Ketchup

Catch the ingredients and have a taste of Guatemalan food!
